# Keyword Research: Hire a Hacker

Source: Google Keyword Planner, account `869-709-9298`, location `United States`, language `English`, network `Google`, date range `Jun 2025 - May 2026`.

Seed terms: `hire a hacker`, `hire ethical hacker`, `ethical hacker for hire`, `account recovery hacker`, `penetration testing services`.

## Strategic Read

The homepage should target `hire a hacker` as the primary keyword because it has clear search demand and matches the domain concept. The page should then immediately reframe the intent as legal hiring: ethical hacker services, account recovery help, and penetration testing services.

The strongest commercial supporting cluster is penetration testing. `penetration testing services`, `penetration testing`, `web application penetration testing`, and vendor/comparison variants all show strong US demand and high top-of-page bids. These should become supporting pages, not all be forced into the homepage.

## Homepage Targeting

- Primary keyword: `hire a hacker`
- Secondary keywords: `hire ethical hacker`, `ethical hacker for hire`, `account recovery hacker`, `penetration testing services`
- Search intent: compare ways to legally hire someone for account recovery help, ethical hacking, cybersecurity investigation, and security testing.
- Angle: "If you searched hire a hacker, here are the safest legal services and sites to compare."

## Supporting Page Plan

1. `/penetration-testing-services/`
   - Target: `penetration testing services`
   - Include: web app, network, cloud, API, external/internal tests.

2. `/best-penetration-testing-companies/`
   - Target: `best penetration testing companies`
   - Include: ranked company/vendor list.

3. `/penetration-testing-cost/`
   - Target: `penetration testing cost`, `penetration testing pricing`
   - Include: cost ranges, scope factors, quote checklist.

4. `/account-recovery-help/`
   - Target: `account recovery hacker`
   - Reframe: legal account recovery help, device cleanup, evidence collection, official recovery steps.

5. `/website-penetration-testing/`
   - Target: `website penetration testing`, `web application penetration testing`
   - Include: business website, WordPress, SaaS, API, and web app security review.

## Keywords To Avoid As Money Pages

Do not build commercial pages around terms that imply unauthorized access, phone spying, password theft, social-media account takeover, stalking, malware, or bypassing login systems. If those phrases appear in research, handle them only in warning, FAQ, or safety sections that redirect users to legal alternatives.
